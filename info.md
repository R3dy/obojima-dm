# The Curious World Within — Complete Adventure Content

## Adventure Overview
- **Title**: The Curious World Within
- **Intended For**: 2 to 4 2nd-level characters
- **Source**: Obojima: Tales From The Tall Grass by 1985 Games
- **Setting**: Okiri Village, Miss Lindley's Workshop
- **Premise**: The adventurers help a young postal knight and find themselves shrunk to the size of a mouse when they infiltrate a witch's workshop in hopes of procuring a letter.

## Adventure Summary
The adventurers decide to help out a young postal knight (Lomi) and find themselves shrunk to Tiny size when they infiltrate Miss Lindley's witch workshop to retrieve a misdelivered letter. As they navigate the strange landscape at mouse-size, they'll fend off bora bugs, handle pixie tricksters, talk to a giant cat, and perhaps even fight a giant Venus fly rat.

## Key NPCs

### Lomi
- 13 year old boy, younger brother of Ashi (a knight of the Courier Brigade)
- Desperately wants to be a postal knight to impress his older sister
- Delivered a letter to the wrong address (Miss Lindley instead of Mrs. Linfrey)
- Offers: a few sea petals, a couple of non-magical First Age trinkets, his services as an assistant, and gold flowers he would earn
- If helped successfully, may ask the party to deliver Mrs. Linfrey's letter (hooks into next adventure)

### Miss Lindley
- Local witch of Okiri Village, resident fixer and mender of goods
- Her workshop is a small and humble wooden house
- The house is magical and invulnerable with various enchantments
- Not present during the adventure (away in town)
- Pixies and her cat Emerson live in the workshop

### Ashi
- Knight of the Courier Brigade, Lomi's older sister
- Gave Lomi his first delivery as a test

### Mrs. Linfrey
- Intended recipient of the letter
- May live in another city (adventure hook)

### Tina and Meena (Pesky Pixies)
- Two giant pixies who live on the workshop main floor
- Tricksy agents of mischief and mayhem
- Sweet when Miss Lindley is around, mischievous when she's away
- Taunt, tease, and lead adventurers astray
- Can also help identify magic trinkets (make stuff up half the time)
- Love to tease Emerson the cat
- Recently hid Emerson's treats atop the bookcase
- Can magically close shutters and douse candles

### Emerson, the Witch's Cat
- Imperious cat with portly paunch, crooked whiskers, thick fur sticking out at all angles
- Always looks like he just woke up
- Has the run of the workshop but prefers hallway and basement
- Uses Lion stats with Intelligence 11
- Strained relationship with pixies — feud over Miss Lindley's attention
- If befriended, barters knowledge about which potion restores size
- Knows what some other items in the workshop do
- If he trusts the party: gives info upfront, wants treats retrieved
- If he doesn't trust: holds back knowledge until treats are safely delivered
- Generally found sleeping atop his cushion embroidered with "Emerson"

### Tabitha Turnip
- Turnip spirit who helps Miss Lindley
- Chronically grumpy and dour
- Only reveals herself to clean up spills, put out fires, deal with chaos
- Does tasks in slow, deliberate manner
- Responds with eye rolls, sighs, sidelong glances of displeasure
- The ONLY one who knows beyond doubt every magic item in the workshop
- Getting her help to identify items is "a task worthy of the greatest of heroes"

## Workshop Description

### Exterior
- Small and humble wooden house in Okiri Village
- Little cobble path to front door painted bright green
- "Closed" sign in faded cursive above door
- Boom Beri bushes on either side (picked clean of fruit)
- Windows frosted over
- Numerous birds nesting on tiled roof
- Padlocked double door to cellar in the back, covered in ivy

### Magical Properties
- House is invulnerable
- Variety of spells and enchantments placed over the years
- No chimney or small entrances besides doors and windows
- Powerful enchantment keeps entrances locked when Miss Lindley is away
- Shrink trap: shrinks all who enter to Tiny size
- Doors/windows shut and re-lock after shrinking occurs
- Cellar doors are non-magical (old delivery entrance)

### Three Ways to Enter
1. **Ivy-covered double door to cellar** (easiest) — non-magical, DC 13 Dex to pick lock, AC 13 to break
2. **Windows** — magically locked, requires dispel magic
3. **Front door** — magically locked, requires dispel magic

## Locations

### The Cellar
- Smells of damp earth and pungent, unrecognizable odor
- Magic flames in soot-caked lanterns on walls
- Old wooden crates and sagging shelves with dust-covered bottles and curios
- Arcane glyphs cover the floor (trigger the shrink effect)
- Serves as catch-all room: old projects, spare supplies, rarely used potion ingredients
- Cat paw prints and claw marks (massive to Tiny creatures)
- Spoiled and unfinished elixirs and potions
- Uncommon ingredient from a region other than Gift of Shuritashi
- Old broken First Age trinket
- If ruckus is made, alerts Emerson the cat

### The Staircase
- Ordinarily normal, now a series of towering steps (~15 feet tall each)
- Leads up to landing piled with books and boxes
- Rickety wooden stairs connecting cellar and main floor
- At top: hallway and Emerson's cushion

### The Hallway (Main Floor)
- Narrow hallway piled with old books, papers, jars, clutter
- To a Tiny creature: perilous like crossing a canyon with unstable cliffs
- Small end table with cage containing a venus fly rat
- Pixies may cause cascade of heavy books to fall
- Emerson may knock books over if startled

### The Bookcase
- Tall bookcase filled with papers, notebooks, dusty tomes about witchcraft
- Contains:
  - 1 cassette with spell "Vegetable Blade" (stored like a scroll)
  - Potion recipe for Elder Elixir (Ingredients: Spindle Leg Spider Webs, Origami Crane, Brush Reed)
  - The letter addressed to Mrs. Linfrey (hidden by pixies)
  - Sachet embroidered with "Emerson" containing pungent cat treats at the top

### The Witch's Work Table
- Large work table covered with tools of Miss Lindley's arcane trade
- Array of tools hanging from walls
- Three areas: Hallway, Bookcase, Witch's Worktable

### Getting on the Table
- Must climb 60-foot (relative) wooden table legs
- Navigate overhang to get onto tabletop
- Climbing speed: no issue
- Others: DC 14 Strength (Athletics) check
- Pixies may cause mayhem during the climb
- DC 14 Passive Perception to notice venus fly rat before it attacks

### Bubbling Cauldron
- Small iron cauldron bubbling over First Age propane burner
- Contains arcane mixture of herbs, liquids, and magic
- Smells aromatic, changes with every inhalation
- 15 feet (relative) rim off tabletop
- Effects if drunk (roll d6):
  - **1**: Everything is Funny (disadvantage on rolls for 1 hour, DC 13 Int to resist laughing)
  - **2-4**: Harness the Wind (feather fall effect for 5 minutes)
  - **5-6**: Touch of Nature (can cast Pillar of Force once, uses highest of Int/Wis/Cha)

## Encounters

### BUG ATTACK!
- While climbing stairs: ambushed by 1d4+1 bora bugs
- Crawl from cracks in the walls
- Alternative: attack after an avalanche in the hallway

### AVALANCHE!
- If books/crates/jars/papers fall
- Creatures in affected area fall prone
- DC 15 Strength save or take 1d10 bludgeoning damage and be restrained

### Venus Fly Rat
- Hiding amongst the mess on the work table
- Originally intended to be used as an ingredient
- Now a dangerous threat
- Attacks when unsuspecting adventurer gets close to center of table

## Monsters

### Giant Bora Bug
- **Size**: Medium Beast, Unaligned
- **AC**: 12 (natural armor)
- **HP**: 22 (4d8 + 4)
- **Speed**: 30 ft., Climbing 20 ft., Flying 30 ft.
- **Stats**: STR 12(+1), DEX 14(+2), CON 13(+1), INT 4(-3), WIS 12(+1), CHA 6(-2)
- **Skills**: Perception +3
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 13
- **Challenge**: 1/2 (100 XP), Proficiency +2

**Traits**:
- Bioluminescent Glow: Emits dim light in 10-foot radius

**Actions**:
- Horn Strike: Melee +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., 5 (1d8+1) piercing
- Spike Flurry (Recharge 5-6): Each creature within 10 ft. must DC 12 Dex save or take 7 (2d6) piercing, half on success

### Giant Pixie
- **Size**: Large Spirit, Neutral Good
- **AC**: 14 (natural armor)
- **HP**: 22 (4d10)
- **Speed**: 30 ft., flying 30 ft.
- **Stats**: STR 10(+0), DEX 18(+4), CON 10(+0), INT 14(+2), WIS 15(+2), CHA 16(+3)
- **Skills**: Perception +4, Stealth +8
- **Senses**: Passive Perception 14
- **Languages**: Sylvan, Common
- **Challenge**: 1/2 (100 XP), Proficiency +2

**Traits**:
- Magic Resistance: Advantage on saves against spells and magical effects
- Innate Spellcasting (Cha, DC 13, +5 to hit):
  - At will: Druidcraft, Jolt, Root Grab, Transparency
  - 1/day each: Confusion, Control Animal, Detect Thoughts, Entangle, Obscure Object, Pacify Person, Sleep, Whelm Weapon

**Actions**:
- Gift of Flight: Grants flying speed 30 ft. to a creature. Lasts while pixie holds breath (1 + Con mod minutes, min 30 seconds). One creature at a time. 1 minute cooldown.

### Giant Venus Fly Rat
- **Size**: Large Beast, Unaligned
- **AC**: 14 (natural armor)
- **HP**: 34 (4d10 + 12)
- **Speed**: 30 ft., Climbing 20 ft.
- **Stats**: STR 15(+2), DEX 15(+2), CON 12(+1), INT 12(+1), WIS 14(+2), CHA 7(-2)
- **Skills**: Stealth +5
- **Damage Resistances**: Poison; Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks
- **Condition Immunities**: Blinded, Charmed, Frightened
- **Senses**: Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 11
- **Challenge**: 1/2 (100 XP), Proficiency +2

**Actions**:
- Snap Jaw: Melee +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., 4 (1d4+2) piercing + Grappled (escape DC 12). While grappled: 4 (1d4+2) piercing at start of each turn.
- Tail Strike: Melee +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., 5 (1d6+2) bludgeoning
- Pollen Burst (Recharge 5-6): 15-foot cone, DC 11 Wis save or Confusion for 1 minute. Repeat save at end of each turn.

## Adventure Conclusion

### Escape!
- If all goes well: adventurers return to normal, retrieve Mrs. Linfrey's letter, escape without a hitch
- Lomi is overjoyed and thanks the party profusely

### Continuing the Adventure
- Lomi could mention the letter still needs delivering
- May ask party to deliver it (Mrs. Linfrey may live in another city)
- Lomi can accompany as NPC
- Sets up next adventure hook

### Mrs. Linfrey's Letter
- If opened: Lomi is horrified, tries to dissuade
- Will report to Courier Brigade (consequences in later adventures)
- Contents could be:
  - Hook into next adventure
  - Confession of love or misdeed leading to a new tale
  - Invitation to a secret witch's costume ball
